Our Education-Related Involvement

by James Smith on Feb 12th 2022


Man's mind, once stretched by a new idea, never regains its original dimensions.



Education lays the groundwork for a better life. It is the most potent social transformation trigger. A youngster, on the other hand, cannot be educated in solitude. Only a strong family, particularly the mother, will be willing to educate their child. As a result, we believe in the lifecycle approach rather than focusing on just one period of a person's life.
Education enables people to earn a living and raises their understanding of a variety of concerns. Smile Foundation works to educate, empower, and grow better citizens in areas ranging from healthcare to good social behaviour to understanding one's rights.
