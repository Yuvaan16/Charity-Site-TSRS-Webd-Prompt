Sangharshseal Jan Kalyan Seva Samiti

“For it is in giving that we receive.”

SJKSS is a charitable organisation which does various kinds of social work in rural areas in North India, particularly in the Delhi NCR region.

Our Aims

  • Sending more than 18 million studnets to back to school
  • Provide good quality roads and houses to more than 60,000 villagers
  • Providing fod for the needy
  • Planting 50 million trees till 2025
  • Providing employment to more than 10,000 people each year
  • Reconstructing ruined areas
