"The SJKSS Foundation team passionately believes in this proverb, as well as the fact that athletes who strive for excellence can inspire many others to do the same. By supporting both able-bodied and differently-abled athletes, we also promote better health and overall well-being for our athletes, a level playing field for all genders, and an inclusive society overall. All of the following are consistent with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals for all member countries, including India."
